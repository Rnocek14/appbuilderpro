## The ten structural gaps (ranked by how much "grail" each unlocks)

**1. Reachability: the brain can touch 14 of its own ~hundreds of capabilities.** The catalog is
the bottleneck of agency ‚Äî everything not in it is invisible to intent. The grail metric:
catalog coverage ‚Üí UI coverage. Program, not project: every new surface ships WITH its action;
the coverage suite enforces it.

**2. Temporal agency: it compiles plans, it doesn't run projects.** Plans execute once, in one
sitting, and die at the approval seam. A chief of staff runs ARCS: durable plans (a table),
waiting states ("paused until the company draft is approved" ‚Üí auto-resume), scheduled
continuations, and monitoring ("the campaign never shipped", "the hunt has been dry for 3
weeks", "Jane's intake stalled at 4/9"). This converts the Orchestrator from a compiler into a
project manager ‚Äî and it is the highest-leverage single build remaining.

**3. Situation, not retrieval: no unified world-model.** Memory recalls similar text; it does
not hold "the state of things": what's live, working, owed, changed, per venture. The pieces
exist (world_intelligence, liveness, momentum, engagements, plans, hunts) but no single
SITUATION object is assembled and consulted by every compile and every Commander turn. Grail:
the orchestrator plans from current reality, not just from the sentence.

**4. Creation stops at the artifact boundary.** The builder creates apps OUTSIDE the OS; Garvis
creates artifacts INSIDE it. The strong form of "creator of everything": creation that EXTENDS
THE CREATOR ‚Äî generated tools mounted as in-system rooms (the wardrobe room), generated
automations installed as standing orders, genesis emitting new area types backed by generated
apps. Garvis growing new organs on demand. Custom Rooms is step one.

**5. Meta-learning: the loops improve the operator's judgment, not the engines.** Verdicts tune
drafting; nothing yet grades the system's OWN output against outcomes: hunts that never yield
should mutate their queries; campaigns with zero engagement should change the strategy stage;
plan sections repeatedly left [YOU FILL] should trigger intake asks. The consolidation loop
proposes lessons about the BUSINESS ‚Äî the next octave proposes revisions to the MACHINE.

**6. Earned autonomy: trust is binary.** Everything outbound needs approval, forever. The grail
is a dial: per-action-class trust the operator raises after a track record ("auto-approve
follow-ups under $0 risk; keep cold pitches manual"), fully audited, instantly revocable. The
approval spine already records everything needed to earn it.

**7. Senses: it perceives a sliver.** Static HTML, search snippets, its own send/reply loop,
what the operator pastes. Missing: rendered-DOM fetch (JS portals), whole-inbox awareness (not
just replies to its own sends), calendar, payments/financial feeds, social listening. A creator
that can't perceive the whole business surface plans from partial reality.

**8. Identity: multi-entity isolation is skin-deep.** Sender identity per world exists; contact
stamping, world-aware sending, per-world inbound, and custom domains don't (fully). "Two
companies, cleanly" remains ~70% true.

**9. Self-proof: it verifies logic, not liveness.** 90 verify suites + CI test the pure cores;
nothing continuously proves the LIVE wiring (a nightly canary: compile a sandbox plan, run a
test hunt against a fixture page, round-trip a test envelope). Grail systems demonstrate their
own health, not just report probe results.

**10. One voice: three context assemblers remain.** Commander, agent runs, and the builder each
see different slices of memory (partially unified this session ‚Äî knowledge now reaches all
three, but mind digest and situation don't). One assembler, one situation, every surface.

## The path (each item unlocks the ones after it)

1. **Durable plans + the project loop** (gap 2) ‚Äî plans table, waiting states, auto-resume
   across approvals, a heartbeat monitor slice that nags stalled arcs. Fixes the genesis seam.
2. **The Situation model** (gap 3) ‚Äî one assembled per-world state consumed by compiles,
   Commander, and the monitor.
3. **Catalog program** (gap 1) ‚Äî systematic sweep: every clickable capability becomes an
   action; contract suite grows in lockstep.
4. **Custom Rooms** (gap 4) ‚Äî embed deployed/preview apps as world-area surfaces; then genesis
   emits room-backed areas.
5. **Earned autonomy** (gap 6) ‚Äî trust ledger per action class + auto-approve thresholds.
6. **Engine self-tuning** (gap 5) ‚Äî outcome-graded hunts/campaigns/plans proposing their own
   revisions through the knowledge gate.
7. **Senses** (gap 7) ‚Äî rendered fetch, inbox ingestion, calendar.
8. **Deep isolation + canary + one assembler** (gaps 8-10).

*The one-sentence summary: the machine now creates, learns, and executes behind honest gates ‚Äî
the grail is a system that runs arcs instead of plans, plans from situation instead of
sentences, extends itself when it creates, earns autonomy instead of asking forever, and proves
itself alive every night.*
